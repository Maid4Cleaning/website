45 Songs to Get You in the Cleaning Groove

Be Swept Away by the Rhythm—and a Sparkling Home!

At Maid 4 Cleaning, we know chores can feel like a drag—but we also know how fast a great playlist can sweep you away. These 45 songs are made to make your mop dance, your broom shimmy, and your duster spin like it’s on Dancing with the Stars.

Whether you’re tidying up or tackling a full house refresh, put this playlist on and let the sparkle begin!

Need help? We’re just a call away in Saint Louis at (314) 626-5885.
✨ Be Swept Away.

🎧 Why Music Helps You Clean Faster

  • Gives you energy when you’re dragging through that laundry pile

  • Lifts your mood (even during deep fridge scrubs)

  • Turns boring tasks into a solo dance party—no judgment here!

🧽 The Maid 4 Cleaning Ultimate Playlist (Now with Extra Sparkle)

  • “Let’s Groove” – Earth, Wind & Fire
