6 Best Places to Donate Clothes and Furniture in Saint Louis

As we head into a new season, it’s the perfect time to refresh your space by letting go of items you no longer use. Donating old clothes and furniture not only clears out clutter—it helps our Saint Louis neighbors and reduces waste in our landfills. At Maid 4 Cleaning, we love a good declutter before a deep clean. Giving back is the first step toward a home that feels light and peaceful.

According to the EPA, Americans generate over 17 million tons of textiles annually, with a shocking 11.3 million tons ending up in landfills. Furniture waste is equally concerning, making up more than 25 percent of what gets tossed. Donating instead of discarding can make a huge difference.

​Donating your gently used clothes and furniture is a wonderful way to declutter your home while supporting the Saint Louis community. Here are six local organizations that will ensure your items find a new home with those in need:​

1. Miriam Switching Post

Best for: Gently used furniture and household items
Why donate here: Proceeds support the Miriam Education Program, aiding children with learning disabilities.
How to donate: Drop off donations at their Brentwood store or schedule a pickup for large items.
Location: 292 Hanley Industrial Court, Brentwood, MO 63144
Phone: 314-962-6059
Website: miriamstl.org​

2. St. Vincent de Paul Society of St. Louis

Best for: Furniture, clothing, and household goods
Why donate here: Your donations support programs assisting individuals and families in need throughout the Saint Louis area.
How to donate: Drop off items at one of their nine thrift store locations or schedule a furniture pickup.
Phone: 314-881-6000
Website: svdpstlouis.org​

3. Home Sweet Home

Best for: New or gently used furnishings and household items
Why donate here: Helps individuals and families transitioning out of homelessness by providing essential household items.
How to donate: Drop off donations at their Maplewood warehouse.
Location: 10 Sunnen Drive, Suite 100, Maplewood, MO 63143
Phone: 314-448-9838
Website: homesweethomestl.org​

4. Charity Clothing Pickup

Best for: Clothing for men, women, and children
Why donate here: Offers convenient pickup services, ensuring your clothing donations benefit local charities.
How to donate: Schedule a pickup or find a nearby drop box location.
Phone: 636-282-0000
Website: clothingpickupstl.com

5. The Salvation Army Thrift Stores

Best for: Clothing, furniture, and household goods
Why donate here: Proceeds from sales fund rehabilitation programs for individuals battling addiction.
How to donate: Drop off items at various locations throughout Saint Louis or schedule a pickup.
Phone: 1-800-SA-TRUCK (1-800-728-7825)
Website: satruck.org​

6. Guardian Angel Settlement Association

Best for: Clothing and household items
Why donate here: Supports programs aimed at breaking the cycle of poverty in Saint Louis.
How to donate: Drop off donations at their location.
Location: 1127 N. Vandeventer Ave., St. Louis, MO 63113
Phone: 314-773-9027
Website: gasastl.org

By donating to these local organizations, you not only declutter your home but also contribute to the well-being of the Saint Louis community.
