8 Smart Things to Look for When Hiring a Cleaning Service

Keeping your home clean shouldn’t be another stressful item on your to-do list. Between work, errands, and kids’ activities, finding time to thoroughly clean can feel nearly impossible. That’s where professional help comes in! But before handing over the keys to just anyone, it’s important to know what to look for in a trustworthy, high-quality cleaning company.

At Maid 4 Cleaning, we’ve been proudly serving the Saint Louis area with eco-friendly, professional house cleaning tailored to busy families and individuals. Here's our expert list of what to consider when hiring a maid service near you:

1. Bonded, Licensed, and Insured – What It Really Means

Always ask if the company is licensed, bonded, and insured. A license shows they’re operating legally. Bonding protects you in case of theft or damage. Insurance covers accidents that might happen during the cleaning. A trustworthy cleaning service will gladly provide this documentation.

2. Read Local Reviews

Don’t underestimate the power of a good review. Platforms like Google, Yelp, and Nextdoor offer unfiltered insight into customer experiences. Look for a company with a consistent track record of reliability, friendliness, and professionalism.

3. Know What Type of Cleaning You Need

Not all cleaning is created equal. Are you looking for a weekly spruce-up, a deep seasonal clean, or help with a move-in/out? Whether you want eco-friendly products or need pet-hair removal, make sure the company offers customizable plans. At Maid 4 Cleaning, we tailor every clean to your needs — no cookie-cutter packages here.

4. Budget Realistically

Hiring a cleaning service is an investment in your time and sanity. While it can be tempting to go with the cheapest option, remember: you get what you pay for. Most Saint Louis homeowners find that biweekly or monthly cleanings are surprisingly affordable — especially compared to what they spend on cleaning supplies.

Quick Math:
The average American spends $40–$50/month on cleaning products alone. That’s over $600/year — enough for regular professional cleaning instead!

5. Check the Service List

Does the company offer more than just a wipe-down? Look for deep cleaning options, organization help, green cleaning, and even laundry services. We offer all that and more — because no two homes (or clients!) are the same.

6. Local Matters

National chains may have name recognition, but local companies like Maid 4 Cleaning offer personalized service, consistent cleaners, and a better understanding of your neighborhood’s needs. Plus, we’re just a call away: (314) 626-5885.

7. Ask About Their Cleaning Products

If you're eco-conscious or have allergies, ask what products are used. We focus on safe, effective, non-toxic products and microfiber cloths (which remove up to 99% of bacteria using just water)​.

8. Background-Checked and Trained Staff

Your home is your sanctuary. You deserve to feel safe. Ensure the company does background checks and trains their team thoroughly. At Maid 4 Cleaning, our employees go through vetting, training, and regular quality checks to maintain our high standards.
