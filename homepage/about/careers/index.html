A Fresh Start Begins Here: Join the Maid 4 Cleaning Family in Saint Louis
At Maid 4 Cleaning, we believe that a clean home is more than spotless floors and tidy countertops. It’s about creating comfort, peace, and the freedom to enjoy life. But behind every shining surface and cheerful client is a dedicated team, and we’re inviting you to be a part of it.

Be Swept Away By More Than Just a Job
Picture this: You start your day with a smile, heading to a beautiful home in the Central West End, sunshine streaming through the windows. You’re not just cleaning; you're creating moments of joy for families, professionals, and retirees who trust you like a member of the family.

That’s the heart of working with Maid 4 Cleaning. Our team isn’t just a workforce—we’re a family. We support each other, grow together, and celebrate the little wins, whether it’s a five-star review or just a job well done.

Why You’ll Love Working With Us
Here’s what makes this more than a job—it’s a lifestyle that works with you:

No Nights, Limited Weekends and Holidays: More time for what matters most—your family, your friends, and yourself.

Generous Pay with Perks: Competitive wages, performance bonuses, and tips that reflect your hard work.

Paid Training & Career Growth: Whether you're starting fresh or looking to level up, we’ll guide you every step of the way. Think Team Lead, Quality Specialist, or even Manager roles.

Use of Company Vehicles: We provide reliable transportation while you're on the job.

Flexible Scheduling Options: Full-time and part-time positions available for all lifestyles.

And yes—we believe in having fun along the way. After all, cleaning may be our job, but joy is our culture.

Stories from the Field
One of our team members, Maria, has been with us for over 10 years. She still talks about the time she decorated a child’s room with a favorite stuffed animal display—and how the delighted giggle from the little one reminded her why she loves what she does.

Whether you’re making a senior feel seen, brightening a family’s day, or just enjoying the satisfaction of a job well done, every shift is a chance to make a difference.
