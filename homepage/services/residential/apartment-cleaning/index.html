Apartment and Condo Cleaning

Life in a Saint Louis condo or apartment should be a breeze—concierge at the door, cafes on every corner, and maintenance just a call away. But even in luxury spaces in neighborhoods like Clayton, Central West End, or Ladue, keeping your home effortlessly clean can be the one missing piece. That’s where Maid 4 Cleaning comes in to help you indeed be swept away.

I’m Ella, and at Maid 4 Cleaning, we understand that your time is precious. Picture returning to a sparkling clean condo, your favorite candles lit, floors gleaming, and every surface dust-free—all without you having to lift a finger. This is the everyday reality our clients enjoy, and we're here to bring this convenience to your life.

What Makes Our Condo and Apartment Cleaning Special?
We’re more than just a mop and a bucket. Our services are tailored for high-end living, and we bring that extra level of polish and care our Saint Louis neighbors expect. Whether you're tucked into a Central Corridor penthouse or enjoying skyline views from a luxury Clayton apartment, we create a customized cleaning plan that reflects your lifestyle.

From one-time deep cleans to weekly refreshes, we offer flexible scheduling that works with your calendar. And yes—we clean inside the microwave, under the couch cushions, and those overlooked baseboards too. Our thorough approach ensures every nook and cranny is spotless, giving you peace of mind.

What We Clean: Every Inch Matters

Kitchen

Sanitize counters and sinks

Degrease stove hoods and wipe cabinet fronts.

Polish stainless steel and appliance exteriors

Empty trash and refresh floors

Bathrooms

Scrub and sanitize tubs, showers, and toilets.

Shine mirrors and faucets.

Wipe down counters and cabinets.

Sweep and mop floors with a fresh scent.

Bedrooms

Dust everything, even the high corners

Vacuum carpets or mop hardwood

Tidy beds (yes, we’ll make them)

Sanitize remotes and light switches

Living Areas

Vacuum under and around furniture

Dust blinds, baseboards, and décor

Mop or vacuum as needed

Wipe down tech and tabletops.

Bonus:

Hosting a party? We’ll prep your space.

Moving in or out? We’ll make sure you leave (or arrive) in sparkling condition.

A Warm Touch for High-Rise Living

Whether you’re a busy professional or want more time to enjoy Forest Park or dinner on Euclid, you can trust us to have your back.
