Holiday Cleaning

Special Event & Holiday Cleaning Services in Saint Louis
There’s something magical about opening your home to loved ones. Whether it’s a glittering holiday dinner in Clayton or a surprise engagement party in Ladue, these special moments deserve a setting that sparkles just as much as the occasion.

At Maid 4 Cleaning, we know that behind every perfect party and cozy holiday gathering is someone (like you) juggling a thousand details. Let us take a few of those off your plate specifically, the dusting, vacuuming, scrubbing, and polishing.

Because You Deserve to Be Swept Away
Hosting in high-style Saint Louis neighborhoods like Town and Country, Frontenac, or Central West End means every detail matters. From gleaming kitchen counters ready for gourmet spreads to spotless guest bathrooms, our team ensures your space feels as welcoming as your company.

We offer customized cleaning for every kind of celebration:

Pre-Event Cleaning: We'll freshen your home top to bottom, including polishing stair rails, staging the kitchen, and making those foyer floors shine for a grand entrance.

Post-Event Cleanup: When the last champagne flute is emptied, we'll handle the mess. We’ll scrub, shine, and reset your space, so all that’s left for you to do is relax.

Holiday Deep Cleans: Before the garlands go up in your University City brownstone or guests arrive in your Chesterfield estate, we’ll ensure everything from windows to baseboards gleams.

New Year Resets: After the glitter settles and the guests are gone, start your year with a clean, calm home and a clear mind.

Cleaning with a Personal Touch
We understand that inviting someone into your home is personal. That’s why we always begin with a conversation. Whether you want your Sub-Zero fridge polished before a dinner party or just need help with the high-traffic areas, we’ll create a tailored plan that fits your lifestyle and schedule.

From large holiday galas to intimate brunches, our flexible scheduling and attention to detail make us a favorite among discerning homeowners in neighborhoods like Westwood, Huntleigh, and the Central Corridor.
