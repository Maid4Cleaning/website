Kitchen Cleaning

Ella Jimenez

There’s something magical about a freshly cleaned kitchen. It’s where your morning coffee brews, where school lunches are made with care, and where friends seem to gather even when a living room is just steps away. At Maid 4 Cleaning, we believe the kitchen is the heartbeat of your home. That’s why our kitchen cleaning services are designed to do more than just tidy up — we aim to breathe new life into your space, so you can get back to what really matters.

What’s Included in Our Saint Louis Kitchen Cleaning Services?
We treat your kitchen like the sacred space it is. Our team thoroughly scrubs and sanitizes all your surfaces — countertops, backsplashes, and cabinet fronts. We use safe, high-quality products that remove buildup and restore shine, leaving your kitchen sparkling and hygienic.

Whether you're hosting Sunday dinners in Ladue or prepping healthy meals in your Clayton kitchen, our cleaning team delivers results that let your space shine.

Appliance Cleaning? We’ve Got That Covered Too
We wipe down and polish the exterior of your major appliances: ovens, refrigerators, dishwashers, range hoods, and more. Inside your microwave? Spotless. Your stainless steel? Smudge-free and gleaming. We take care of greasy fingerprints and crumb-filled crevices so you don’t have to.

Floors That Shine in Every Home
Your kitchen floors take a daily beating from spills, crumbs, and foot traffic. We sweep and mop with care, using floor-specific solutions and paying extra attention to corners where grime loves to hide.

The Finishing Touches
We empty trash bins, dust blinds and baseboards, straighten chairs, and even clean the window above your kitchen sink. These thoughtful touches make your kitchen feel freshly polished and fully cared for.

Custom Kitchen Cleaning for Your Lifestyle
Whether you’re managing a bustling family life or simply want to enjoy a consistently clean space, we’ll tailor your cleaning schedule to fit your needs. Weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly — our service is a perfect fit for busy households in Ladue and Clayton who want a spotless kitchen without lifting a finger.

Why Saint Louis Families Choose Maid 4 Cleaning
We’re not a franchise — we’re a locally owned and operated business right here in Saint Louis. Our clients trust us because we bring reliability, attention to detail, and genuine care to every clean.

We specialize in recurring home and kitchen cleaning services for homeowners who value their time and appreciate a sparkling space.

Be Swept Away
With Maid 4 Cleaning, it’s not just about wiping down surfaces. It’s about creating a space where you can cook with joy, laugh with family, and unwind with peace of mind. Let us do the scrubbing so you can make the memories — minus the mess.

Call today and let your kitchen sparkle like never before — we’re proud to serve homes in Ladue, Clayton, and surrounding Saint Louis communities.
