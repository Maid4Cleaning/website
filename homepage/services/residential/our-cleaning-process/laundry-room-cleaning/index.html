Laundry Room Cleaning

Ella Jimenez

At Maid 4 Cleaning, we believe every corner of your home should feel like a breath of fresh air — even your laundry room. That’s right, Ladue, Clayton, and Town and Country families, we’re taking a look at the most overlooked room in your house and giving it the makeover it deserves.

Picture this: You’ve just finished a long day. You wander into your laundry room to tackle a load of towels, only to find dust bunnies by the baseboards, lint coating the dryer, and a stale odor that no lavender detergent can mask. Sound familiar? That’s where we come in.

Our residential housekeepers don’t just sweep and dash. We bring intention and care to every space, especially the laundry room, where cleanliness can significantly impact the freshness of your clothes. With years of experience and some tricks straight from the homekeeping legends, we offer more than just a quick wipe.

What’s Included in Our Laundry Room Cleaning Service:

Thorough wiping of washer and dryer surfaces to remove dust, detergent drips, and mystery smudges.

Sanitizing the sink and faucet — yes, your utility sink deserves love too.

Mopping floors to shine, whether they’re made of tile, laminate, or wood.

Dusting cabinets and shelving, including behind that one detergent bottle from 2021.

Emptying lint bins and wastebaskets, as dryer lint builds up quickly and can pose a fire hazard.

Bonus Touches Available:

Want your bed linens changed or laundry folded and sorted? Just ask. Our team can handle light laundry tasks to save you even more time, perfect for those busy days in Frontenac or Creve Coeur.

Why Our Clients in Saint Louis’s Finest Neighborhoods Love It

Laundry rooms often fall into the “I’ll get to it later” category, but a clean, organized space makes laundry feel less like a chore and more like a calm moment in your day. Imagine stepping into a laundry room that smells fresh, looks spotless, and is free of the grime that builds up from everyday use. It’s a minor upgrade that brings immense comfort.

A tidy home reduces stress and builds pride. And that includes your laundry nook. You shouldn’t dread the space you use so often.

Let Us Create Your Laundry Haven

At Maid 4 Cleaning, we don’t just clean — we care. From luxury homes in Des Peres to stately estates in Westwood, we craft our services to meet your needs and your lifestyle. We customize every clean and take note of your preferences.

Whether you book weekly, bi-weekly, or for a seasonal refresh, we’re here to help Saint Louis shine — one laundry room at a time.
