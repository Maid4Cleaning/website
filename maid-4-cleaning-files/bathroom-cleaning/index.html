Bathroom Cleaning

Ella Jimenez

There’s something magical about walking into a freshly cleaned bathroom — the kind of magic that happens when the mirrors sparkle, the air smells crisp, and every surface looks like it’s been kissed by sunlight. But let’s be honest — between busy workweeks, soccer practices, and just trying to keep up with life, most of us don’t have the time (or let’s face it, the motivation) to scrub grout or chase dust bunnies behind the toilet. That’s where we come in.

At Maid 4 Cleaning, we believe everyone deserves a clean, stress-free home, starting with the bathroom. After all, it’s where you start your day, wind down your evening, and escape for five peaceful minutes with the door locked. Our Saint Louis house cleaning services specialize in turning your bathroom into a space that feels not just clean, but comforting.

What Makes Our Bathroom Cleaning Shine?
Bathrooms are hardworking spaces, and we treat them with the attention they deserve. When our professional house cleaners in Saint Louis arrive, we don’t just tidy up — we deep clean every surface using eco-friendly cleaning products that are powerful on grime and gentle on your home.

Here’s what’s included in our detailed bathroom cleaning services in Saint Louis:

Spotless Showers and Tubs: We’ll remove soap scum and shampoo residue so you can finally enjoy that bubble bath without side-eyeing the tiles.

Sanitized Sinks and Faucets: From toothpaste splatter to hard water marks, we’ll leave every faucet and fixture gleaming.

Toilet TLC: We disinfect inside and out for a truly fresh finish — because your throne deserves royal treatment.

Crystal-Clear Mirrors: We’ll banish water spots and fingerprints for a mirror that’s ready for your best selfie.

Fresh Floors: Swept, mopped, and sanitized for that barefoot-friendly feel.

Wastebasket Emptied, Surfaces Dusted: We take care of the little things too — baseboards, shelves, light fixtures, and more.
