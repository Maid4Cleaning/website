About Maid 4 Cleaning

We are a local St. Louis cleaning company focused on consistency, professionalism, and clear cleaning systems. We’re growing and looking for dependable cleaners who want stable hours and a supportive team environment.

Position Summary

Clean residential homes and small commercial spaces using our step-by-step system. Work in teams, travel to job sites, and complete tasks with quality and efficiency.

What You’ll Do

  • Clean homes and commercial spaces using company checklists
  • Work in 1–2 person teams
  • Communicate through our field software
  • Travel to job locations in St. Louis and University City
  • Provide high-quality, detail-focused cleaning services

What We Offer

  • Full-time and part-time schedules
  • Paid hands-on training
  • Consistent weekly hours once trained
  • Supplies, tools, and cleaning system provided
  • Supportive leadership and growth opportunities


Qualifications

  • Enjoys active, physical work
  • Able to follow checklists and cleaning systems
  • Can commute to job locations
  • Detail-oriented and dependable
  • No experience required — we train you
