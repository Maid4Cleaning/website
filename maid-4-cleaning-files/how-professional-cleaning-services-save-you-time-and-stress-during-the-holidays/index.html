Why a Clean Home Matters More During the Holidays

A clean home isn’t just about aesthetics—it’s about creating a welcoming, safe, and healthy environment for you and your guests. According to The Science of Cleaning, cleaning removes visible dirt and invisible allergens that can trigger discomfort during indoor gatherings​.

With unpredictable weather and muddy boots in St. Louis winters, routine cleaning becomes essential. Our professional team knows exactly how to deep-clean high-traffic areas, freshen up guest bathrooms, and restore kitchen shine—so you don’t have to.

You deserve to be swept away by the season—not buried in chores.

Quick Tips to Keep the Holiday Mess at Bay

Even if you're doing some DIY cleaning between visits, here are a few pro tips:

  • Entryway control: Place washable mats inside and outside your doors to trap snow and salt.
  • Dealing with holiday spills: Red wine? Blot immediately, then use a mix of hydrogen peroxide and dish soap (test first!). Don’t use vinegar here—it can set the stain.
  • Kitchen grease control: Use a degreaser—not baking soda and vinegar—on stovetops. Baking soda is mildly abrasive, but it needs a surfactant (like dish soap) for grease.

Need more help? We’ll handle the hard stuff, like grout lines and appliance fronts, so your home shines top to bottom.
