Move-In Move-Out Checklist


🚚 Move-Out Cleaning Checklist


Purpose: Reset the home for inspection or new residents. Clean top to bottom, room by room.



🔄 Whole Home



  • ☐ Dust ceiling corners and edges

  • ☐ Clean ceiling fans, vents, and light fixtures

  • ☐ Wipe switches, outlets, and doors (front/back)

  • ☐ Clean windows, sills, and tracks

  • ☐ Wipe blinds and shutters

  • ☐ Wipe baseboards and trim

  • ☐ Spot clean walls (marks, smudges)

  • ☐ Vacuum carpets and rugs

  • ☐ Sweep and mop hard floors



🍽️ Kitchen



  • ☐ Clean inside/outside refrigerator (remove drawers/shelves)

  • ☐ Clean oven (racks, door, stovetop burners)

  • ☐ Clean microwave inside/out

  • ☐ Clean dishwasher front panel

  • ☐ Wipe cabinets and drawers inside/out

  • ☐ Wipe backsplash and counters

  • ☐ Clean sink and faucet

  • ☐ Clean vent hood/filter

  • ☐ Sweep and mop floor

  • ☐ Remove all trash



🚿 Bathrooms



  • ☐ Dust ceiling, vents, and lights

  • ☐ Clean mirrors and glass

  • ☐ Scrub sink, faucet, and vanity

  • ☐ Scrub shower/tub, walls, doors, and grout

  • ☐ Scrub toilet including hinges and base

  • ☐ Wipe cabinets/drawers inside/out

  • ☐ Wipe doors, blinds, windows, baseboards

  • ☐ Sweep and mop floor



🛏 Bedrooms



  • ☐ Dust ceiling, fans, and lights

  • ☐ Wipe furniture if present

  • ☐ Clean doors, windows, sills, and blinds

  • ☐ Wipe baseboards and trim

  • ☐ Vacuum carpets or mop floors

  • ☐ Clean inside closets and shelves



🛋 Living & Dining Areas



  • ☐ Dust ceiling, fans, and lights

  • ☐ Clean windows, blinds, and tracks

  • ☐ Wipe all surfaces and baseboards

  • ☐ Vacuum furniture and floors

  • ☐ Mop hard flooring



🧺 Laundry / Utility Areas



  • ☐ Dust ceiling and lights

  • ☐ Wipe washer/dryer exterior

  • ☐ Clean sink/faucet if present

  • ☐ Wipe cabinets/shelves

  • ☐ Sweep and mop floor



🏚 Basement



  • ☐ Remove cobwebs from ceilings, joists, corners

  • ☐ Dust/wipe light fixtures and exposed pipes

  • ☐ Wipe baseboards, window ledges, and sills

  • ☐ Clean basement windows and tracks

  • ☐ Wipe utility sink and faucet

  • ☐ Wipe furnace, water heater, softener exteriors

  • ☐ Sweep stairs, landings, and floor

  • ☐ Mop or damp mop floors

  • ☐ Vacuum/wipe shelves and under stairs

  • ☐ Clear and bag any debris or trash



✅ Final Walkthrough



  • ☐ Appliances off and clean

  • ☐ All trash removed from property

  • ☐ Floors vacuumed/mopped last

  • ☐ Lock doors, turn off lights

  • ☐ Take photos of finished job
