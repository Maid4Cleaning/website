Your Ultimate Guide to an Organized Garage

When was the last time you took a good look at your garage? If it's like most in Saint Louis, it might be storing more chaos than cars. According to the National Association of Productivity & Organizing Professionals, nearly 50% of homeowners claim the garage is the most disorganized space in their home. At Maid 4 Cleaning, we know a cluttered garage can become a frustrating mess. But with the right approach, it can transform into a practical, tidy extension of your home.

Step 1: Declutter First

Before diving into organizing, start with a deep clean. This means:

  • Set aside a full day or weekend to tackle the garage.
  • Use three categories: Keep, Donate/Sell, and Toss.
  • Be honest about what you really use. If it’s been untouched for years, it’s probably time to let it go.

Step 2: Know What NOT to Store in the Garage

St. Louis weather ranges from freezing to humid, making the garage a poor place for sensitive items. Avoid storing:

  • Propane tanks
  • Wine or alcohol
  • Photographs and documents
  • Cardboard boxes (opt for plastic instead)
  • Clothes and linens

Step 3: Smart Storage Solutions on a Budget

We believe in budget-friendly transformations. Here are practical solutions:

  1. Use Vertical Space: Install pegboards or wall-mounted hooks for tools, sports gear, and gardening supplies.
  2. Get It Off the Floor: Use shelving, ceiling racks, or hooks to clear floor clutter.
  3. Clear Bins with Labels: See what you store and make it accessible for the whole family.
  4. Repurpose What You Have: Old bookshelves, crates, and jars make great DIY storage.
  5. Drawer Units for Small Parts: Store nails, screws, and other small hardware in labeled mini-drawer systems.

Step 4: Make It a Routine

Keeping your garage clean is easier once it's organized. Schedule a monthly 15-minute tidy-up to sweep, restack, and relocate as needed.

Bonus Tip: Add a Drop Zone

Create a small area by the garage door with hooks, a bench, and a shelf for shoes, bags, and jackets. It's especially helpful for busy families!
